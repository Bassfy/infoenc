FAQ

Common
questions.

How long does a typical project take?

Most web projects take 6–12 weeks. Mobile apps typically run 12–20 weeks. SaaS platforms vary widely based on complexity — we'll scope this during the discovery phase.

What's your minimum project budget?

Our minimum engagement starts at $10,000 for focused work like landing pages or design systems. Most full projects run between $25k–$150k+.

Do you work with early-stage startups?

Absolutely. Some of our best work has been for pre-seed and seed-stage founders. We can help you move from idea to MVP faster than you'd expect.

Do you offer ongoing retainer support?

Yes. After project delivery, we offer monthly retainer packages for continuous development, feature additions, and performance optimization.

Who owns the code at the end?

You do. 100%. Full IP transfer is included in every contract. Your code, your product.
