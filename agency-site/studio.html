Our Story

Born from
discontent.

VOREX was founded in 2017 by a group of engineers and designers frustrated with the status quo — agencies shipping mediocre work at premium prices, startups launching products that collapsed under load, enterprises stuck in decade-old tech.

We set out to prove that speed and quality aren't mutually exclusive. That beautiful design and bulletproof engineering can coexist. Eight years and 120+ projects later, we're still proving it every day.

Today we operate from San Francisco, London, and Singapore — a fully remote-first studio working with founders, enterprise teams, and everyone in between.
